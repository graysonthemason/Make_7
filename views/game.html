Object:

  • Be the first player to have your color number tiles add up to 7—in a straight line—either vertically, horizontally or diagonally!

Playing:

  • On your turn choose a number above the column you'd like to place your tile. Clicking "Play" will insert your tile in the corresponding column. "3's" can only be placed in the red boxes.
